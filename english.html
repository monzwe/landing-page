Free hikes on the Salève

Every Sunday, hikers meet at 10:00 am at the terminus of bus 8 in Veyrier-Douane , 100 meters from the customs on the Swiss side. A representative of the Association Genevoise des Amis du Salève (AGAS) will be waiting for you in any weather. No need to sign up in advance. Depending on the number of volunteer guides present, several itineraries may be proposed. Depending on the chosen circuit, count on 5 to 8 hours of walking, including 3 hours of ascent at a steady pace (800 meters of altitude difference). Departure on foot from the meeting point. You will need proper hiking shoes, snow spikes in winter if icy, hat and sun cream in summer, one or two hiking poles (optional), waterproof jacket, passport, money, picnic and drinking water.

Please note:

You need to be in good physical condition and wear appropriate footwear (no sandals, flip-flops, etc!) The Salève is a real mountain. Also be sure to bring enough water, as there are no sources along the hiking paths

By joining, you release AGAS from all responsibility in case of accidents. We are not professional mountain guides. We just use this platform to bring people with similar interests together and to enjoy nature. Join at your own risk. You are responsible for your own safety, and for having adequate insurance cover for anything that might happen to you during this event.
