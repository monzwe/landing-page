Free hikes on the Salève

Every Sunday, hikers meet at 10:00 am at the terminus of bus 8 in Veyrier-Douane , 100 meters from the customs on the Swiss side. A representative of the Association Genevoise des Amis du Salève (AGAS) will be waiting for you in any weather. No need to sign up in advance. You will need proper hiking shoes, snow spikes in winter if icy, hat and sun cream in summer, one or two hiking poles (optional), waterproof jacket, passport, money, picnic and drinking water.

Depending on the number of volunteer guides present, several itineraries may be proposed. Depending on the chosen circuit, expect 4-6 hours of walking, including 2-3 hours of ascent at a steady pace (800 meters of altitude difference). Departure on foot from the meeting point.

Additional hikes

Sometimes we organise an additional hike the same day from a different meet-up point to explore the South of Mont Salève. These hikes tend to be longer, and we will be far from the cable car, for experienced hikers only. Details are announced here one or two days in advance.

Please note:

You need to be in good shape and wear appropriate footwear (no sandals, flip-flops, etc!) The Salève is a real mountain.
Also be sure to bring enough water, as there is no drinking water along the trails.

The association declines any responsibility in case of accident.
